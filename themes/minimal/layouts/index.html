Introduction to Eastinco Consulting

Welcome to Eastinco Consulting Limited. We solve our customer's business and technical problems for their product development (hardware, firmware and software). As experts in real time embedded C++ and .NET, we help developers by mentoring, training, and supporting the development process. We help non-developer firms by writing software for them or helping them manage their projects. We program in a variety of languages, but especially in C++. We provide architectural vision and inspiration, manage projects, provide business and management advice.

Some recent projects in 2020:

  • Developed C++ low power (LoRa) wireless application on STM32 processor module.
  • Developed firmware for wireless system using long range and short range 433HZ transceivers.
  • Developed C# application in .Net Core and then ported into embedded Linux.
  • Developed real time video application in C++11 for object detection using machine learning platform Darknet YoloV3.
  • Deployed data recording and video analyzing application into cloud via AWS EC2 and S3 with docker.

What we do

We provide consulting service to help our clients solve their business problems. Often, our contribution is in the form of programming, project management technical writing, or training. Other times it's a feasibility study to help map out a path the client can follow. We also provide mentoring to small developer teams to help them adopt new technologies. These days people ask us for advice about C++ a lot, or moving from a Windows-only world to a cross-platform world. We're following the latest developments for our clients and breaking new ground in this exciting wave of technology development.

Our history and people

Eastinco Consulting was founded in 2017 as a software development and consulting firm. One of our founding partners, Qiang Yu, is our chief mentor with over 15 years of software development experience. Our other founding partner, Yue Zheng, also has over one decades of software development experience and handles our programming contracts, system administration and much of our general consulting. In the past, we've had a number of other employees and associates. At the moment we are content with just the two partners, and some associates available to us when we need more horsepower on a particular project.

Where we are

Our office is in Bristol, UK.  We get into European when we need to -- and Bristol has an airport that can get us anywhere.
